在商业街上，你会看到许多不同的店铺、商家和顾客。通常包括：

1. **零售店**：如服装、鞋类、饰品、化妆品等商店，提供最新的时尚商品。  
    **Retail Stores**: Shops selling clothing, shoes, accessories, cosmetics, etc., offering the latest fashionable items.
    
2. **餐饮店**：包括快餐店、咖啡馆、餐厅等，提供各类食物和饮品。  
    **Restaurants**: Fast food joints, cafes, and restaurants offering various food and drinks.
    
3. **服务商店**：如理发店、美甲店、修理店等，提供日常生活的服务。  
    **Service Stores**: Barbershops, nail salons, repair shops, etc., offering everyday services.
    
4. **娱乐设施**：如电影院、游戏厅、书店等，提供休闲娱乐体验。  
    **Entertainment Facilities**: Cinemas, arcades, bookstores, etc., providing leisure and entertainment experiences.
    
5. **街头摊贩**：售卖小吃、饰品、玩具等，通常价格较为亲民。  
    **Street Vendors**: Selling snacks, accessories, toys, etc., typically at affordable prices.
    
6. **广告和促销活动**：展示品牌宣传、折扣信息等，吸引顾客。  
    **Advertising and Promotions**: Displays of brand ads, discount offers, etc., attracting customers.
    

有价值的东西可能因人而异，但通常包括：

- **新颖和高质量的商品或服务**，它们能满足个人需求或提升生活品质。  
    **Novel and high-quality products or services** that meet personal needs or improve quality of life.
    
- **体验和互动**，例如参加活动、发现新品牌、享受休闲时光等。  
    **Experiences and interactions**, such as attending events, discovering new brands, enjoying leisure time, etc.
    
- **品牌和声誉**，一些商店和品牌因其信誉而被视为值得信赖，可能具有更高的价值。  
    **Brands and reputations**, where some stores and brands are trusted for their credibility and may be considered more valuable.
    
- **人际交流和社会互动**，商业街是人们社交的场所，能带来有意义的互动和连接。  
    **Social interactions and connections**, where commercial streets serve as places for people to socialize and form meaningful connections.
    

从效用主义的角度来看，你可以评估这些物品或体验是否能带来长期的快乐或减少痛苦，从而确定它们是否有价值。  
From a utilitarian perspective, you can evaluate whether these items or experiences bring long-term happiness or reduce suffering, thus determining their value.
