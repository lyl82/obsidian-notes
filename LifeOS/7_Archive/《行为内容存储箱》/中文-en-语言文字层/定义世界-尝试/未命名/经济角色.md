当然，以下是按照您要求的格式，使用换行来分隔中文解释和英文解释的经济角色中英文对照及解释：

1. 用户 (User)
   - 指使用产品或服务的个人，他们可能是最终消费者，也可能是企业用户
   - An individual who utilizes a product or service. They can be end consumers or business users.

2. 顾客/消费者 (Customer/Consumer)
   - 直接购买商品或服务的个人
   - An individual who directly purchases goods or services.

3. 客户 (Client)
   - 与企业有业务往来的个人或组织
   - An individual or organization that has a business relationship with a company.

4. 买家 (Buyer)
   - 在市场上寻找并购买商品或服务的个人或企业
   - An individual or business that looks for and purchases goods or services.

5. 卖家 (Seller)
   - 提供商品或服务以换取货币或其他物品的个人或企业
   - An individual or business that offers goods or services in exchange for money or other items.

6. 零售商 (Retailer)
   - 直接向最终消费者销售商品的商家
   - A business that sells goods directly to the end consumer.

7. 批发商 (Wholesaler)
   - 向零售商或其他商人销售商品，通常以较大数量和较低价格
   - A business that sells goods to retailers or other merchants, usually in larger quantities and at lower prices.

8. 分销商 (Distributor)
   - 负责将产品从制造商运输到零售商或最终消费者的中间商
   - A middleman who coordinates the transportation of products from manufacturers to retailers or end consumers.

9. 代理商 (Agent)
   - 代表客户进行购买或销售商品和服务的个人或公司
   - A person or company that represents clients in buying or selling goods and services.

10. 中介 (Broker)
    - 在买卖双方之间提供服务，帮助交易的完成
    - A person or company that facilitates transactions between buyers and sellers.
