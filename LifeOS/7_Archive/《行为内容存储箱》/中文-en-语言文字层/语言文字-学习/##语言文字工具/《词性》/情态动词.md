动词原形一起构成谓语。
表达能力、意愿、推测的工具
- **表示能力：** can, could (e.g., I can speak English.)
- **表示许可：** can, could, may, might (e.g., May I come in?)
- **表示可能性：** can, could, may, might (e.g., It may rain tomorrow.)
- **表示必须和需要：** must, need (e.g., You must finish your homework.)
- **表示建议和义务：** should, ought to (e.g., You should study harder.)
- **表示意愿：** will, would (e.g., I will help you.)
- **表示推测：** must, may, might, could (e.g., He must be very tired.)
